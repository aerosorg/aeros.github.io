About AEROS

We're an open innovation community advancing AstroTech, Aerospace, and science education. Our mission is to empower future explorers and researchers with hands-on tools and real impact.

  • Launch-ready student projects
  • Community-powered innovation
  • Research & learning by doing
  • Global collaboration for local change
